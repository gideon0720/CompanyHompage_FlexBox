The PlayHub

Engineering play hub is a place for curious enthusiasts of engineering simple to complicated things. These interested individuals come together as they work on projects in school or just to concretize their ideas.

The PlayHouse

Engineering play house is a place to introduce the concept of building ideas and things by catering to little enthusiasts. As the students grow into their competence, they move from the Playhouse to Play hub where they get to interact with people who build and manufacture things.
